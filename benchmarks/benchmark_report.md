## Interpretation and limitations

These are reference measurements for the public seasonal-naive baseline on synthetic data. They are reproducible engineering evidence, not claims of performance on a real utility grid. Runtime varies by hardware and excludes HTTP, network, serialization, and UI overhead. Trained LSTM, GRU, Transformer, and XGBoost models require dataset-specific evaluation before comparison.
